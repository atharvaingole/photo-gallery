Photo Gallery Of Esperanza & Aashayein-2K23

Traditional day

Traditional Day is observed as a celebration of India's diverse culture, and is usually relaxed day at work. It's a day designated for people to come in traditional attire from their home state, or specific culture of their choice. Everyone at the college came dressed in their best ethnic attires.

Bollywood day

prof. ram meghe institute of technology and research celebrated Bollywood Day, where students dressed as “iconic” characters from Bollywood films. Bollywood Day is celebrated in college as a way to showcase and celebrate Indian cinema and culture. In addition to being a fun and festive event, Bollywood Day can also promote cultural exchange and understanding, as it allows students from diverse backgrounds to come together and appreciate a shared cultural experience.

Mismatch day

The aim was to enable the students to understand the concept of proper dressing and according to the occasion. students came up with new and creative ideas using their imagination and wore clothes to reflect their mismatch personalities. They wore different pairs of socks and shoes.

Signature Day

The final-year students of PRMIT&R, celebrated their last moments together by organizing a 'Signature Day'. The campus saw students writing messages and sketching smileys and caricatures on each other's faces and clothes before parting ways.
